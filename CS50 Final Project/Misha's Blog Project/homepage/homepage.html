I studied Political Science and Russian at the University of Vermont before getting my master's degree in Russian and post-Soviet politics at University College London. I've traveled throughout Russia, as well as Eastern and Central Europe. I've created this blog in order to keep my writing skills up to par, to talk about my travels, as well as my experience with the Russian language. Davayte!
